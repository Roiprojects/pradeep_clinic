The Blueprint of Development

Growth tracking is foundational to pediatric health. At Dr. Pradeep's Clinic, we meticulously monitor your child's physical growth parameters—including height, weight, and head circumference—ensuring they are on the right clinical curve for their age and genetics.

Our developmental milestone assessment goes beyond physical metrics, checking for early identification of cognitive, motor, and social delays to provide timely intervention and expert guidance.

We believe that healthy growth is the best gift for your child's future. Our standardized assessments provide a holistic view of your child's development, combining clinical data with personalized parenting advice.

Milestone FAQs

Common indicators and questions for developmental tracking.

What happens if my child is behind a milestone? expand_more
Every child develops at their own pace. A delay doesn't always mean a problem, but it indicates a need for closer clinical observation. Early identification allows us to implement simple interventions that can make a huge difference.
How often should growth be monitored? expand_more
For infants, monitoring is usually done during every vaccination visit. For older children, a dedicated developmental check-up every 6 months to a year is recommended to maintain a consistent record.
Does genetics affect milestone timing? expand_more
Yes, genetics play a role in physical growth (like height) and sometimes in the timing of certain milestones. We factor in family history during our clinical assessments to give a nuanced view of your child's progress.
