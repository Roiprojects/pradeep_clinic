Frequently Asked Questions

How does consultation work?

After booking, you will receive appointment confirmation and guidance from the clinic team. You can discuss your child’s concerns, symptoms, reports, and receive professional pediatric advice.

What do I need for the consultation?

Please keep your child’s previous medical records, prescriptions, vaccination details, and any relevant reports ready for better evaluation.

Is consultation suitable for my child’s condition?

Consultations are suitable for newborn care, general child health concerns, vaccinations, growth monitoring, follow-ups, and guidance for common pediatric conditions. Emergency cases may require immediate hospital care.

How long is each consultation?

Initial consultations usually take around 10–15 minutes, depending on the child’s condition and discussion needed.
