Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your personal and medical information with the highest standards of confidentiality.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Dr. Pradeep Children's Clinic. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our patients and their families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

To provide high-quality pediatric care, we may collect various types of information, including:

  • Personal Identification: Name of parent/guardian and child, date of birth.
  • Contact Information: Phone number, email address, and residential address.
  • Health Data: Clinical history, vaccination records, and growth details provided via inquiry forms.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and usage patterns through cookies.

3. How We Use Information

Your information is used strictly for professional medical and administrative purposes:

  • To schedule and manage clinic appointments.
  • To respond to your inquiries regarding pediatric services.
  • To maintain accurate medical records for ongoing care.
  • To improve our website experience and clinical service quality.

4. Data Protection

We implement robust physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect your child's data. All digital clinical records are stored on secure servers with restricted access to authorized medical personnel only.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality. Cookies help us understand how you interact with our site, allowing us to provide a more personalized and efficient user experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may be affected.

6. Third-Party Sharing

Dr. Pradeep Children's Clinic does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data is only shared with authorized healthcare partners (e.g., diagnostic labs) when essential for your child's treatment, or when required by law.

7. User Rights

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to request access to the information we hold about you or your child. You may also request corrections to any inaccuracies or, in certain circumstances, ask for the deletion of personal data from our non-medical records.
